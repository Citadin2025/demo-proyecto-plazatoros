History

Inaugurated in 1910, the Plaza de Toros Real de San Carlos was part of an ambitious tourism project in Colonia. After decades of abandonment and the passage of time, the emblematic building was restored, recovering its splendor. Today, it combines its imposing history with a new role: being a stage for shows, culture, and events that look to the future.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where is the Plaza de Toros located?

    It is located in Real de San Carlos, a few minutes from the historic center of Colonia del Sacramento, Uruguay. Access can be by vehicle, bicycle, or even walking from the coastal rambla.

    What activities or services are currently offered?

    Currently, the Plaza de Toros has been restored and functions as a cultural, tourist, and event space. Guided tours, shows, exhibitions, and educational activities are held, in addition to having tourist information services.

    How can I get more information or make a reservation?

    You can communicate through the official channels of the Intendencia de Colonia or visit the official Plaza de Toros website, where current schedules, prices, and activities are published.

    Is the Plaza de Toros accessible for people with reduced mobility?

    Yes. The restoration of the building included criteria for universal accessibility, including ramps, elevators, and appropriate signage, so that all people can enjoy the tour safely and comfortably.

    Can private or institutional events be held at the Plaza de Toros?

    Yes. The space can be rented for cultural, business, or institutional events, provided it is coordinated in advance with the administration. The Plaza offers modern infrastructure and a unique historical setting for holding special activities.

    Location of the Plaza

    The Plaza de Toros Real de San Carlos is located in the Real de San Carlos neighborhood, west of Colonia del Sacramento, Uruguay. It is about 5 minutes from the historic center, on the Rambla Costanera, surrounded by a natural environment and with easy access from the main avenues of the city.
